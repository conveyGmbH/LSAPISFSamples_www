Custom fields must follow Salesforce naming conventions: use only letters, numbers, and underscores, and ensure the name is unique within the same object. In the API, custom field names are identified by a suffix of two underscores followed by a lowercase "c". For example, a custom object labeled "Issue" appears as Issue__c, and a custom field labeled "Hire Date" appears as Hire_Date__c.
